Remember


the sky that you were born under, know each of the star's stories.
the moon, know who she is.
the sun's birth at dawn, that is the strongest point of time.
sundown and the giving away to night.
your birth, how your mother struggled to give you form and breath. You are evidence of her life, and her mother's, and hers.
your father. He is your life, also.
the earth whose skin you are: red earth, black earth, yellow earth, white earth brown earth, we are earth.
the plants, trees, animal life who all have their tribes, their families, their histories, too. Talk to them, listen to them. They are alive poems.
the wind. Remember her voice. She knows the origin of this universe.
you are all people and all people are you.
you are this universe and this universe is you.
all is in motion, is growing, is you.
language comes from this.
the dance language is, that life is.
